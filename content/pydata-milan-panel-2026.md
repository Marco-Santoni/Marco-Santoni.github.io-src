Title: Panel at PyData Milan: Managing Teams, Stakeholders and Delivery in the GenAI Era
Date: 2026-03-22
Status: published

<img src="../images/pydata-milan-2026/panel.jpg" width="400" />

I had the pleasure of being a guest panelist at [PyData Milan](https://www.meetup.com/pydata-milano/events/313716178/) on March 18, 2026. The event was hosted at TeamSystem's office in Milan.

The panel was about **Managing Teams, Stakeholders and Delivery in the GenAI Era**. Together with [Parvaneh Shafiei](https://www.linkedin.com/in/parvaneh-shafiei/) (AI Manager at TUI Musement) and [Alberto Danese](https://www.linkedin.com/in/albertodanese/) (Head of Data Science & Advanced Analytics at Nexi), we discussed topics ranging from data and AI governance, to shipping AI solutions in large enterprises, to how GenAI and coding agents are transforming team organization.

It was a great conversation, and I enjoyed sharing experiences and perspectives with the other panelists in front of the PyData Milan community.
